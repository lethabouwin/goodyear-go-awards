Here's how it works

Sell a 17"+ tyre and claim it on the website (goodyearawards.co.za). Every qualifying 17"+ Goodyear tyre sold and claimed will count towards your dealership’s ranking on the leader board.

The 5 dealerships with the highest quantities sold at the end of the week will a Sportmans Warehouse voucher worth R1100.

Terms & Conditions

  • The top 5 dealerships with the most tyres sold for the week (starting on the 23rd of October, ending on the 27th of October) will receive a Sportsman's Warehouse voucher worth R 1100 for the dealership.
  • All sales must be claimed on the Goodyear Go Awards website in order to count.
  • Only 17-inch tyres and above qualify for the sprint.
  • Only sales done between the 23rd and 27th of October 2023 will count.
  • Each sales executive must load their sales on their own profile.
  • All claims are counted per dealership.
  • The leaderboard is updated daily with the previous day’s sales.
  • Only qualifying Goodyear Tyres will count.
  • Only claims with invoices attached will count.
  • Goodyear has the final decision on the winners.
